Top Destinations in Surigao del Sur

Explore nature's wonders, beaches, and surf spots

Laswitan Lagoon: Nature's Wave Pool

Laswitan Lagoon in Cortes is a unique natural attraction where waves crash over rock formations, creating a stunning saltwater lagoon. It's a must-visit for nature enthusiasts.

Cagwait White Beach: A Tropical Paradise

Often compared to Boracay, Cagwait White Beach boasts powdery white sand and calm turquoise waters. It's a serene getaway for those seeking relaxation.

Lanuza Surfing Spot: Ride the Waves

Lanuza is a haven for surfers, offering world-class waves and a laid-back vibe. Whether you're a pro or a beginner, this spot is perfect for catching waves.
