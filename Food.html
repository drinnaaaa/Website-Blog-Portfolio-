Top Foods in Surigao del Sur

Local delicacies from Lanuza, Cagwait, and Laswitan Lagoon

Lanuza

Inun-unan na Isda (Fish Stew)

Simmered fish in vinegar, garlic, and ginger a classic coastal comfort dish.

Kinilaw na Isda (Raw Fish Salad)

Fresh fish marinated in vinegar, chili, and lime perfectly refreshing after surfing.

Grilled Pusit (Squid)

Fresh squid grilled to perfection and served with spicy vinegar sauce.

Cagwait

Tinolang Isda

A hearty fish soup made with ginger and leafy greens simple and nourishing.

Sea Urchin (Tuyom)

A rare delicacy enjoyed fresh or marinated with vinegar and chili.

Lato Salad (Sea Grapes)

Salty and crunchy seaweed served with tomatoes and vinegar light and healthy.

Laswitan Lagoon (Cortes)

Sutukil (Sugba-Tula-Kilaw Combo)

Enjoy grilled, soup-based, and raw seafood dishes in one local favorite platter.

Adobong Pusit

Squid cooked in soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic savory and tender.

Cassava Delicacies

Sweet native treats like cassava cake and pichi-pichi, made from grated cassava and coconut.
